About Me

I am Bilal Awad, Im a recently graduated Electronics Engineer with a Double Degree, holding a Master’s in Electronics Engineering from Politecnico di Torino and an Engineering Degree (5 years) in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Lebanese University.

My academic and professional journey covers FPGA-based neural network acceleration, control systems, power systems, robotics, renewable energy, and advanced control systems. My thesis focused on implementation of neural networks on FPGA with High-Level Synthesis, achieving real-time performance while reducing energy consumption for edge AI applications.

Alongside engineering, I am passionate about teaching. I’ve guided both school and university students globally, including those at Cardiff, Liverpool, Sheffield Hallam, and Swansea Universities in the UK — with a success rate of over 95%.

Recognized achievements include ranking 1st in Power Automation and 12th in the Lebanese Physics Olympiad. Beyond academics, I am a black belt in Taekwondo, reflecting my discipline and persistence.

My mission is to apply my expertise in electronics, AI, and FPGA design to create innovative and sustainable technologies that make a real-world impact.

"Engineering for me is about building solutions that make technology smarter and life better."
